    Augustus

    (He/Him)

    Imperator Caesar Divi Filius Augustus

    Rome, Italy

    About

    Resilient and visionary leader who transformed the Roman Republic into a thriving and enduring empire. With a deep commitment to stability and prosperity, I implemented critical reforms, expanded territories, and fostered a legacy of enduring governance.

    Experience

    Consul of the Roman Republic: (43, 33, 31-23, 5, 2 BC)

    • Collaborated with fellow statesmen to navigate the complex political landscape, ensuring the stability of the Roman Republic during turbulent times.
    • Negotiated important treaties and alliances, contributing to the diplomatic strength of the Republic.

    Triumvir: (43-27 BC)

    • United the then Republic under the rule of its three most influential leaders

    Pontifex Maximus: (12 BC-Death)

    • Served as the chief priest of the Roman state religion, overseeing religious ceremonies, rituals, and the spiritual well-being of the empire.

    Education

    Received tutelage and guidance from the renowned Julius Caesar, who recognized my potential and contributed to my understanding of governance, strategy, and leadership. Also attained fluency in Greek and Latin, as well as skills in oration, under the nurture of my mother.

    Accomplishments

    • Established the Pax Romana: a 200 year period of relative never before seen peace and stability.
    • Reformed the legal system through codifying roman laws and ensuring consisten justice.
    • Won the Battle of Actium to defeat the combined forces of a triumvir and the Ptolemaic Empire.

    Tiberius

    (He/Him)

    Imperator Caesar Divi Augusti Filius Augustus

    Rome, Italy

    About

    A Roman statesman and military leader who served as the second Emperor of the Roman Empire from 14 to 37 AD. I dedicated my life to the service of Rome and its people, striving to maintain stability and further the legacy of the Julio-Claudian dynasty.

    Experience

    Consul of the Roman Republic: (13, 7 BC)

    • Collaborated with fellow statesmen to navigate the complex political landscape, ensuring the stability of the Roman Republic during turbulent times.
    • Negotiated important treaties and alliances, contributing to the diplomatic strength of the Republic.
